Principal software engineer at [[Amazon]] who collaborated with Filipe on [[Predictive Demographics]] (PD) launch and debugging.

## Role in Predictive Demographics

**Late 2022:** Brought on to salvage the failing Predictive Demographics project (Wibble) alongside Filipe after being reassigned from a separate project (BYOA). As principal engineer, Evan provided senior direction and oversight while Filipe executed the heavy lifting.

**Contributions:**
- Helped diagnose and articulate codebase quality issues in Wibble (untyped JSON, poor model architecture)
- Jointly debugged the failed first launch and root cause analysis (COE investigation)
- Reviewed and guided the Kassandra rewrite approach
- Oversaw the initial shadow publishing and dial-up mechanism design

**Working Style:** Evan stepped back from day-to-day once the refactored pipeline stabilized, moving to oversight mode. Filipe transitioned to leading the work solo, building dial-up infrastructure and developing the launch strategy.

## Context

By the time Filipe relocated to the US team (2023), Evan's direct involvement diminished as Filipe became the primary technical owner of PD. Filipe later led the team's evolution, while Evan moved on to other initiatives.
