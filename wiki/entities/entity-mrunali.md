SDM (Software Development Manager) on the Demographic Targeting team at [[Amazon]], overseeing Filipe and the broader product roadmap.

## Leadership Approach

**Strengths:**
- Hands-on SDM eager to build quickly and deliver results
- Supportive of Filipe's proposal to relocate to the US and lead the team
- Delighted by Filipe's initiative and vision
- Delegated team management and product direction effectively

**Challenges:**
- Complex personality; described as having "a good base" but "corrupted" by organizational pressures
- Can retreat to defensive behaviors when questioned on competence or authority
- Caught between executive pressure (from Ravi/Rohan) and team needs

## Role in Scaling

As SDM, Mrunali:
- Hired and onboarded the US-based Demographic Targeting team (Filipe, Hunter, Vlad, Beena, Vishnu, Harish)
- Supported Filipe's elevation to Science Manager role
- Collaborated on product roadmap and vision documentation
- Coordinated cross-team offsites and communications

## Peer Feedback

Multiple team members (including Filipe) noted that Filipe was often deferred to in technical and strategic decisions over Mrunali's official authority, suggesting strong peer respect despite the formal hierarchy.
