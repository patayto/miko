Apple provides both hardware and subscription services across the author's ecosystem.

## Hardware Services

[[AppleCare+]] offers accidental damage and loss coverage for individual devices:
- AirPods: $14.99/month
- Watch: $3.99/month
- iPhone: $13.49/month (covers theft and loss; highest per-device cost)

International coverage is a question: whether [[AppleCare+]] remains valid outside the US requires verification, as does the viability of generic tech insurance as a cost-saving alternative.

## Subscription Services

**[[iCloud+]]**: Cloud storage subscription available at two different price tiers across the author's accounts—£8.99/month (UK account) and $9.99/month (US account). Both offer 2TB storage. Renewal dates differ (July 21st vs August 7th), suggesting unneeded duplication requiring consolidation.

**Apple Music**: $10.99/month streaming subscription.

**Claude Pro**: $20/month LLM access (though Claude Pro is Anthropic, not Apple, it appears to be subscribed via the patrick.laverty@icloud.com account).

## Account Surface

Multiple email addresses with Apple services create consolidation opportunities and security surface area; the author maintains both patlav@live.co.uk and patrick.laverty@icloud.com with overlapping subscriptions.
