A brief, introspective note capturing core questions about personal direction and identity. The note frames work not as a job but as the larger project of building something meaningful, asking four fundamental questions: what to build, how to build it (alone or with others), which kinds of collaboration matter, and who the author wants to become in the process.

The piece moves from external direction ("what am I going to do?") through collaborative preference ("with people: yes") to deeper identity work ("who do I want to be(come)?"). It assumes that work is foundational to identity, and that choosing both the what and the with-whom are primary identity decisions.

This reflects ongoing exploration into [[Values Alignment]], [[Authentic Wants]], and the relationship between daily work and long-term becoming. The brevity suggests this is a question-holding piece rather than an answer-yielding one—a checkpoint in active inquiry rather than a resolved stance.
