A comprehensive personal productivity system designed to balance focus on meaningful goals with necessary maintenance and rest. The framework combines daily task prioritization, weekly rhythms, and value alignment to create sustainable high performance without burnout.

The system rests on several interconnected components. **Daily structure** uses a tiered task hierarchy: Big Wins (2–3 major topic-based objectives), Maintenance Tasks (recurring administrative work), and Micro-tasks (quick wins under 5 minutes). This prevents both scope creep and the false productivity of only tackling small items.

**Weekly rhythm** allocates time across three domains using flexible ratios rather than rigid daily targets: 50% Necessities (essential life maintenance), 30% Growth (skill development, projects), and 20% Relaxation (rest, hobbies). The framework acknowledges that some days will be skewed (e.g., 80% maintenance on cleaning day, 80% growth on hobby projects), but the week balances out.

**Time management techniques** include [[Time Blocking]], [[Task Batching]], [[Habit Stacking]], and working with [[Ultradian Rhythms]] to schedule breaks strategically. [[Time Caps]] prevent tasks from expanding indefinitely, while [[Automate, Eliminate, Delegate]] principles reduce friction. [[Completion Psychology]] and "good enough" standards fight perfectionism.

**Structural elements** include daily [[Morning Routine]] and [[Evening Routine]], plus systematic approaches to reducing decision fatigue through [[Task Prioritization]].

Undergirding the whole system is [[Values Alignment]] — the framework reflects personal [[Values]] including honesty, humility, perseverance, resilience, flexibility, and humour. Regular attention to [[Shadow Work]] helps maintain integrity as the system evolves.
