# Subscription and Account Audit

This journal entry documents a systematic audit of active and cancelled subscriptions across multiple accounts and providers, with particular focus on [[Apple]] services and identifying consolidation opportunities.

## Account Landscape

The author maintains multiple email addresses with overlapping service subscriptions:
- **patlav@live.co.uk**: Primary account with Dashlane Premium and Google One Storage
- **patrick.laverty@icloud.com**: Secondary Apple-centric account with duplicate [[iCloud+]] subscription

This duplication creates both billing waste and account security surface area. A key issue: two separate [[iCloud+]] subscriptions with different renewal dates (July 21st vs August 7th) that may be redundant.

## Active Services

[[Apple]] services dominate the subscription landscape across both accounts: [[iCloud+]], [[Apple Music]], [[AppleCare+]] coverage for multiple devices (AirPods, Watch, iPhone with theft/loss protection). The iPhone [[AppleCare+]] is particularly expensive at $13.49/month.

Other active services: Claude Pro, Dashlane Premium, Google One Storage.

## Consolidation Opportunities

Several recently cancelled services (Calm, Duolingo, Headspace) suggest the author is rationalizing subscription spend. Two additional issues surfaced:

1. **Veo**: Cancelled but user is unable to log into app (no longer has US phone number), creating recovery friction
2. **Instacart+**: Apparent billing discrepancy—Rocket Money shows $9.99 charge on July 2nd despite account showing yearly subscription cancelled with expiration Jan 2026
3. **Toyota**: Possible duplicate payment after vehicle sale; requires customer support verification

## Open Questions

Whether [[AppleCare+]] coverage remains valid internationally after relocation, and whether generic tech insurance offers better value than maintaining multiple device-specific plans.
