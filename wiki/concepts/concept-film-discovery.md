A system for staying aware of upcoming films that match personal interests. Rather than passively scrolling streaming platforms or relying on algorithmic recommendations, film discovery enables active tracking of specific creators and media properties.

**Filtering dimensions:**
- **Actors:** Monitor all upcoming projects from favorite actors
- **Directors:** Track filmography and upcoming work from preferred directors
- **Genres:** Filter to genres of interest (sci-fi, indie, documentary, etc.)
- **Franchises:** Stay informed of sequels, spin-offs, and universe expansion

**Discovery signals:**
- Theatrical release dates and availability
- Streaming platform availability and release windows
- Festival premieres and special screenings
- Critical reception and reviews

**Related to broader interest management:** A specialized aspect of the [[Interest Tracker]] system, focusing on film rather than music. The same pattern of aggregating scattered awareness into a unified dashboard applies—pulling data from IMDb, streaming platforms, festival databases, and news sources into a single view.
