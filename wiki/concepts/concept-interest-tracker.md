A unified dashboard for monitoring interests across creative pursuits. The tracker centralizes awareness of activity (tours, releases, social posts, upcoming films) from multiple independent sources—streaming platforms, ticketing systems, social media, fan databases—into a single interface.

**Core problem:** Favorite creators and media are scattered across disconnected platforms. Staying aware requires checking Instagram, tour sites, streaming release calendars, and film databases separately. The Interest Tracker consolidates this scattered awareness into one place.

**For music:** Users register favorite artists and receive alerts for:
- Tour dates and venue information
- New music releases and drop dates
- Social media activity (Instagram posts, stories, announcements)
- Festival lineups

**For film:** Users track upcoming releases filtered by:
- Favorite actors and directors
- Genres and franchises
- Release dates and platform availability

**Integration patterns:** The tracker pulls from external sources via APIs ([[Setlist.fm]] for tours, streaming APIs for releases, social APIs for activity) and surfaces notifications through the home page interface.

**Related systems:** Similar to [[Spotify Social Playlists]] in connecting creator activity to user awareness, but broader in scope (not limited to music curation).
