The practice of tracking and receiving notifications about activity from favorite music artists. This includes tour announcements, album releases, social media updates, and live performance schedules.

**Key signals monitored:**
- **Tour dates:** Concert venue, date, and ticket availability via sources like [[Setlist.fm]]
- **Release dates:** New album, EP, or single announcement and drop dates
- **Social media:** Instagram posts, stories, and account activity
- **Festival lineups:** Announcement of artist presence at upcoming festivals

**User benefits:**
- Never miss a tour announcement in your region
- Get first access to presale tickets
- Stay informed of new music from curated artists
- Reduce friction of checking multiple platforms manually

**Technical foundation:** Artist monitoring typically requires integration with:
- Tour database APIs ([[Setlist.fm]])
- Streaming platform APIs (Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music)
- Social media APIs (Instagram)
- Festival/event databases

A purpose-built [[Interest Tracker]] system would aggregate all artist monitoring signals into a unified dashboard for the user.
